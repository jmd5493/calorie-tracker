Motivation for this project:

  • Apply Web Development Concepts

    Use HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Flask, and jQuery to build a functional web application.

  • Learn Full-Stack Development

    Gain experience integrating front-end and back-end technologies with a database.

  • Promote Healthy Eating Habits

    Encourage users to track their meals and make healthier food choices.

  • Provide a Simple and Accessible Tool

    Offer an easy-to-use platform for tracking daily calorie consumption.
